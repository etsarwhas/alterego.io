A Few Facts About Me...

      I love poetry
      Living in the South of France
      I drink my coffee black
      I love to paint portraits and landscapes
      I have two cats named Edgar and Maya

      Id rather spend my days in a cafe than anywhere else
      I get inspiration from people I meet and the places I go.
      I'm a freelance poet who's in no rush. I look up to poets
      such as Edgar Allen Poe, Walt Whitman, Slyvia Plath and
      Maya Angelou. As Honore de Balzac has said before me,
      "As soon as coffee is in your stomach,there is a general
      commotion. Ideas begin to move... similes arise,
      the paper is covered. Coffee is your ally and writing ceases
      to be a struggle."
